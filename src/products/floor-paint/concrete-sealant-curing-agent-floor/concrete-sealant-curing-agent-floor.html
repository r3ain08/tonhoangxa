    Concrete Sealant Curing Agent Floor

    Inquiry Now Live Chat
    Share with:
    Describe
    Product formation
    Concrete sealant curing agent is a concrete seal hardener, it is a colorless transparent liquid material. it can be permanently sealed concrete, in line with all VOC rules.
    Product features
    1. Permanently increase strength and hardness;
    2. Permanent seal impermeability, no dust, hardening wear;
    3. Resistance to chemical attack;
    4. Does not produce dust;
    5. Anti-aging performance;
    6.Construction of convenient, environmentally friendly process;
    6. Save a lot of maintenance and maintenance costs, without waxing, just water mopping the floor.
    Using
    For the indoor and outdoor wear-resistant carborundum floor, terrazzo floor, puree ground floor, flat floor, ordinary cement floor, stone and other basic surface, suitable for the factory floor, warehouse, supermarkets, terminals, airport runway , Bridges, roads and other cement-based place.
    Operational principle
    Concrete seal curing agent through the effective penetration of 3 ~ 5mm, and concrete in the calcium oxide, water and concrete hardening process compounds generated by chemic al reactions, resulting in a hydrated calcium silicate, commonly known as snow silica stone, so that concrete Become a dense whole. The strength and density of concrete are enhanced by increasing the concentration of xygophyllite. At the same time, the crystals produced by the snowy silica gel in the concrete pores effectively block the pores in the concrete and reduce the flow path of the water, thereby preventing the solid and liquid impurities from entering the interior of the concrete, increasing the corrosion resistance of the concrete to the chemical, but While allowing free flow of gaseous water, so that the concrete to maintain "breathing" function. While the ground to produce a higher gloss, increase the use of the aesthetic effect.

    Not applicable:
    1.The concrete seal curing agent is not recommended for use in long-term acid environments.
    2.Treated with colored acrylic coloring or epoxy covering, since the film on the surface will prevent the penetration of the sealant unless the coatings have been completely worn or have been removed by chemical or mechanical means.
    3.Marble, ceramic, glass, metal and so on
    Construction technology:
    1. Polished surface
    2. Clean the base surface;
    3. Spraying concrete sealing curing agent;
    4. Cleaning the base surface;
    5. Preliminary polishing;
    6. Fully polished;
    7. Clean the floor.
    PS: The details about the construction:
    New pouring of the overall powder surface floor construction:
    1. At least 3 days of curing, preferably more than 7 days.
    2. Low pressure sprayed concrete sealant curing agent (standard dosage: 0.1kg/㎡), followed by microfiber mop or broom broom material evenly distributed, do not accumulate material.
    3. The surface of the floor shall remain wet for 15 to 20 minutes.
    4. The floor which is porous floor and has been drying need to spraying again.
    5.Once the ground begins to harden, do not use microfiber mops or brooms.
    6. Drying Time: 2-4 hours
    Construction of ground which polished surface of concrete floor：
    1.Grinding and polishing to 200 mesh (wet or dry grinding)
    2.First concrete seal curing agent material
    3.Grinding and polishing to 400 mesh (wet or dry grinding)
    4.Grinding and polishing to 800 mesh (dry grinding)
    5.Second way concrete seal curing agent material
    6.Grinding and polishing to 1500 mesh (dry grinding)
    7.Grinding and polishing to 3000 mesh (dry grinding)
