    New special antiskid deck paint

    Inquiry Now Live Chat
    Share with:
    Describe
    Composition
    It is composed of resin, modified resin, weather resistant (filling) material, quartz sand, toughening agent, auxiliary agent and solvent
    Advantage
    . Excellent weather resistance, abrasion resistance and slip resistance;
    . impact resistance, Oil resistance
    . Strong adhesion, chemical resistance
    Using
    It is specially used for antirust and anti-corrosion of ship deck parts of all kinds of steel and cement. It is also suitable for anticorrosion coating on offshore working platform
    Packing
    Paint: 18kg/bucket Curing agent: 3kg/bucket

    Surface treatment:
    The surface of iron and steel is treated by sandblasting or shot blasting to reach grade Sa2.5 or manual derusting grade ST3 after coating shop primer
    Matching paint:
    1, new type special ship deck paint primer
    2, slip coating of the middle coating into 100-200% (300 mesh) quartz powder for 2-3 coating
    Deck paint:
    Deck primer 2 layers + deck paint topcoat 2 layer
    Anti-slip paint:
    Deck primer 2 layers + middle coating 2 layers+ deck topcoat 2 layers
    Construction condition:
    The temperature of the base material should be higher than 3 degrees above the dew point. The relative humidity should not be greater than 85%.. The outdoor construction should not be carried out when the temperature is lower than 5 degrees. The construction is strictly prohibited under fog, rain, snow and windy weather.
    In order to make the two components fully crosslinked and cured, with the paint can be ripened 2h at 25-40 ℃, and then spray. If the dry environment is below 22 ℃ without heating and drying conditions, may be appropriate to extend the curing time.
    Construction method:
    Non air spraying: Recommended nozzle: No. 19-23； gas pressure：200-240kg/c㎡
    Brush or roller coating：application
    Conventional spray painting：Not recommended
