frequently asked questions
Can we visit your factory?
You are Warmly welcomed to visit our factory, it would be our great pleasure
What payment way do you accept?
We normally provide the TT, Western Union to arrange the payment. T/T 30% deposit, the balance 70% T/T paid before shipping. If the have special remarks, you can contact and communicate with our sales for better support!
What packaging of the products?
Our common package are 4L and 18L drum. But the products could be packaged according to your requirement.
Sample is free?
Yes, you only pay the shipping charges.
Can you produce the product according to customer’s requirements?
Yes, OEM is welcome. Yes, we could packing the paints according to your request, add the LOGO.
Could you send me all your catalog and price list?
As we have more than thousands of items, even 1 item have different size and package, it is really too hard for us to send you all our catalog and price list for you. Please inform me the items, size and packages you interested in, so we can offer you the price list you want for your reference.
I don’t know which type to choose, can you give me some suggestions ?
Yes, please tell us what the paint will be used for building, then we’ll recommend the most suitable one for you.
