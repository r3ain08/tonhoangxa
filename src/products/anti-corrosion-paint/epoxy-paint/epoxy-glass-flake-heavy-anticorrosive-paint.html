    Epoxy Glass Flake Heavy Anticorrosive Paint

    Inquiry Now Live Chat
    Share with:
    Describe
    Product formation
    The synthetic resin as main film material, thickness of the glass as the aggregate, then add additives and additives agent, Composition of a thick slurry without solvent paint
    Product features
    . The permeability of the medium, gas, water vapor is small, not easy to produce medium diffusion, can effectively avoid the corrosion, bubbling, stripping and other physical damage.
    . Hardening shrinkage rate is small, strong weathering resistance.
    . The resin is hard to age because of the surface micro bending, which can reflect ultraviolet light
    . Excellent resistance to water vapor permeability, construction is easy and simple, easy to repair
    Using
    Used for steel pipe, steel structure, storage tanks and containers, also as wear-resistant, anti slip and abrasion resistance of concrete
    Packing
    Paint:20kg/bucket Curing agent: 4 kg/bucket

    Surface treatment:
    All surfaces to be coated should be clean, dry and free of contamination. Before painting, all surfaces should be root iso8504:2000 standards for the assessment and treatment.
    Construction condition:
    The temperature of the base material is not less than 0℃, at least 3℃ than the air dew point temperature.Relative humidity less than 85%, Fog, rain, snow, wind and rain is strictly prohibited under construction.
    Construction method:
    Spraying: non air spraying or air spraying. High pressure non gas spraying.
    Brush / roll coating: recommended for a small area, but must be in order to achieve the specified dry film thickness.
