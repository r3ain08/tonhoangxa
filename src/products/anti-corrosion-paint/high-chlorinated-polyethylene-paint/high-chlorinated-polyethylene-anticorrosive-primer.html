    High Chlorinated Polyethylene Anticorrosive Primer

    Describe
    Product formation
    The high chlorinated polyethylene resin as anti-corrosion material, and add advanced anticorrosive pigment, solvent, anti rust pigment, stuffing and assistant.
    Product features
    . Film sealing performance is good, fast drying
    . Construction at low temperature,（-10℃-50℃), and easy maintenance
    . Resistance to the atmosphere, water and oil, weather resistance, ozone resistance
    . Film durability, heat resistance, coating tenacity, mildew
    . Good mutual solubility new coating, excellent interlaminar adhesion, so working without stripping the old coating
    . Long storage period, no crust, no caking
    Using
    Used for protecting the surface of marine, port, railway facilities, offshore oil production platform, highway bridge, petroleum, chemical, metallurgy, iron and steel equipment, containers, pipelines, wood and concrete , plant structure, so that it from the atmosphere, water, acid, salt, oil, chemical gas and other corrosive media.
